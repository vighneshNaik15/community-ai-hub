## 📌 Problem Statement

Rural communities in India face significant challenges in accessing:
- 🌾 Real-time market information and fair pricing for agricultural products
- 🏥 Quality healthcare services and government health schemes
- 🎓 Skill development opportunities and education guidance
- 💡 Startup guidance and entrepreneurship support
- 📋 Government scheme awareness and application processes

---

## 💡 Our Solution

A **unified AI-powered platform** that integrates three service domains through a centralized hub, providing personalized recommendations in **Hindi, Marathi and English** through web and IVR phone interface.
